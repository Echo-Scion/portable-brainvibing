# Tactical Viral Growth Blueprints

## 📱 TikTok / Reels / Shorts (Short-Form Video)
- **Hook Engineering**: Master the 3-second pattern interrupt.
- **Retention Body**: Rapid cuts, instant value, no fluff.
- **Loop Architecture**: End with a seamless transition back to the start.
- **Algorithmic Escalation**: Hijack trending audios and use high-emotion topics.

## 🐦 Twitter (X) Engagement
- **Thread Hook Formula**: `[Result + Struggle + Promise]`. 
  - *Example*: "I built a $10k SaaS in 24 hours. I failed 14 times before this. Here are the 5 architectural secrets I learned."
- **Outbound Hijacking**: Identify Tier-1 accounts. Spend 5 minutes on high-value replies rather than screaming into the void.
- **Conversion Trap**: Optimize the Bio and pinned tweet. Use "Build in Public" vulnerability.

## 🤖 Reddit Community Building
- **Trojan Horse Strategy**: 98% pure value post (tutorial/cheatsheet) + 2% organic product mention.
- **Cultural Audit**: Use community jargon. Never use corporate copywriting.
- **Spam Avoidance**: Avoid generic CTAs. Focus on solving specific community complaints.
